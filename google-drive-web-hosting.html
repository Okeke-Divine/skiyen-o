Step By Step Hosting Of Website With Google Drive
Step 1:

Create a google account for your website

Then sign in to google drive with that account (drive.google.com)

Step 2:

Next, we drop the website folder in Google Drive preferably in the root. The folder should start with www. and end with .com

The folder can be created in google drive or uploaded from your device

All your website files should be uploaded to this folder

Step 3:

The first thing you need is an index.html file (Homepage).

Step 4:

Make your folder public

Right click on the folder and click on share

Then change the setting from private to public

Make the setting under public to be viewer

Then click ok (save)

Step 5:

Go to your browser and tyoe DRW DV

Select Google Drive

Select your account for the website

Click Allow

You will be taken to your Admin Panel

Your will see the url of your website

Copy it


You have succesfully hosted your Website

You can now share the url


Advantages Of Hosting With Google Drive

1. It is free (It saves hundreds of dollars per year in web hosting)

2. It is Secure (https)

3. No time down

4. No bandwidth limit


Disadvantages Of Hosting With Google Drive

1. You can only use html, css and js language other languages like php will NOT work.
